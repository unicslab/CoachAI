# Privacy Policy

**CoachAI — Real-Time Intelligent Tennis Coaching System**

---

## Overview

CoachAI is developed and maintained by the Ubiquitous Networking and Intelligent Computing System (UNICS) Lab at the New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how CoachAI handles your information.

---

## Information We Collect

### Camera Data
CoachAI requests access to your device camera solely to perform real-time stroke analysis and movement tracking. **Camera data is processed entirely on-device and is never stored, transmitted, or shared.**

### Usage Data
CoachAI may collect anonymized usage data such as lessons completed, XP earned, and badges achieved. This data is stored locally on your device and is used only to track your personal progress within the app.

### Advertising Data (Google AdMob)
CoachAI uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain device information and identifiers to serve relevant ads. This includes:
- Device advertising identifier (IDFA)
- IP address
- General device information

For more information on how Google handles data, please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers.
- We do not require account registration or login.
- We do not store or transmit camera footage or images.
- We do not sell your data to any third party.

---

## On-Device Processing

All AI inference and performance analysis in CoachAI runs entirely on your device. No video, images, or sensor data from your coaching sessions are ever sent to external servers.

---

## Third-Party Services

CoachAI integrates the following third-party service:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Google AdMob | In-app advertising | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |

---

## Data Retention

CoachAI stores progress data (XP, badges, completed lessons) locally on your device. This data is removed when you uninstall the app. We do not retain any data on external servers.

---

## Children's Privacy

CoachAI is rated 9+ and is not directed at children under the age of 9. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** any data stored locally on your device at any time.
- **Delete** all app data by uninstalling CoachAI.
- **Opt out** of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings (Settings > Privacy > Tracking on iOS).

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of CoachAI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
